Best Spaghetti Sauce

spaghetti sauce with ground beef

A classic, hearty spaghetti with ground beef is a savory Italian-American food, featuring browned ground beef simmered in a rich tomato base with onions, garlic, and herbs like organo and basil

INGREDIENTS

  • Meat
  • Aromatics
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs and Seasoning
  • Fat

STEPS

  1. Brown the Beef: In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook ground beef over medium-high heat until browned and crumbly, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Add diced onions (and bell peppers, if using) to the beef. Cook until vegetables are tender (about 5 minutes). Add minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute until fragrant.
  3. Drain Fat: If the beef is greasy, drain the excess fat to ensure the sauce isn't too oily.
  4. Add Tomatoes & Seasoning: Stir in the tomato paste, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and seasonings (oregano, basil, salt, pepper, sugar).
  5. Simmer: Bring to a low boil, then reduce heat to low, cover partially, and let it simmer for at least 30 to 60 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve: Taste and adjust salt and pepper if needed. Serve over cooked spaghetti with parmesan cheese.
