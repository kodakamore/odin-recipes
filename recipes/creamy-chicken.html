Creamy Chicken Dish

Creamy chicken tikka masala

Creamy Chicken Tikka Masala is a beloved, rich Indian-inspired dish featuring tender, smoky, yogurt-marinated chicken chunks (tikka) simmered in a velvety tomato-based sauce masala

INGREDIENTS

  • Protein
  • Marinade
  • Sauce Base
  • Flavoring/Cream
  • Kasuri methi
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Cashew Paste

STEPS

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine the chicken with all marinade ingredients in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  2. Sear the Chicken: Heat 1 to 2 tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken in batches to avoid crowding, cooking for 3 to 5 minutes until browned and charred in spots. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté Aromatics: In the same pan, add butter and sauté the onions until soft and golden (about 5-8 minutes). Add the ginger, garlic, and spices (garam masala, cumin, coriander, paprika) and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Simmer Sauce: Pour in the tomato puree/passata and add a splash of water if needed. Simmer for 10 to 15 minutes until the sauce thickens and the raw tomato smell is gone.
  5. Finish the Curry: Reduce heat to low, stir in the heavy cream and return the chicken to the pan. Simmer for another 8-10 minutes until the chicken is cooked through. Season with salt and add sugar to balance the acidity, if needed.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh chopped coriander (cilantro) and serve with basmati rice or naan bread.
